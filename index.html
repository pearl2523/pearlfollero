PEARL'S LOVELY HOBBY COLLECTION

A Collection of heartfelt crafts and timeless passion

ABOUT

Hi, Im Pearl Anne Follero! my hobbies has always been a big part of my life, from experimenting with make-up to crafting with crochet, calligraphy, and sewing. Each hobby tells a story, shaped by my experiences and the little moments that inspire me. This space is where i share my passion, hopping to spark creativity in others too!

MY HOBBIES

SEWING

Sewing is my absolute favorite hobby. a true labor of love that connects me with my family's legacy. I first learned the basic when i was in 1st year highschool through our TLE class, but my passion really ignited when u repeated grade 11 and pursued dressmaking. Inspired by the tradition of sewing in my family, my grandparent, my mother, and my father all honed this skill. I began crafting a variety of pieces, from a pajama to a classic Filipiniana, dresses skirts, and blouses. Although some of my earlier projects are now just a fond of memories, my well-kept sewing machine still buzzes with requests from my family for both hand-stitched and machin-made creations.

CROCHETING

Crocheting became my creative escape during grade 9 when the pandemic hit. In our Handicraft class, I struggled with project plans, and the lockdown boredom nudged me to explore a forgotten stash of acrylic yarn and crochet hooks. Initially reluctant, I soon discovered that crochet offered endless possibilities‚Äîfrom cozy accessories to fun home decor. Some of my favorite projects include crocheted flowers, keychains, and easy-to crochet crop tops, each a small celebration of creativity during challenging times.

DOING MAKE-UP

Makeup, to me, is more than just a beauty routine‚Äîit's an art form that allows me to express my personality and creativity. I started getting interested in makeup during the pandemic, although I began wearing it when I was 15. My mom noticed my constant experimentation and, upon returning from abroad with an assortment of products, introduced me to a broader world of possibilities. Between 2021 and 2023, I embraced the half-glam look, but now I lean towards a clean girl or soft glam style, partly because I have fewer products. Each phase of my makeup journey has taught me new techniques and inspired unique styles

EXPLORING MUSIC FROM K-POP TO OPM

One of my hobbies is listening to music and supporting my favorite idols, whether in K-pop, P-pop, or OPM bands. I enjoy listening to songs by artists like DWTA (Jhasmine Villanueva), Donnalyn Bartolome, and Janella Salvador, as well as K-pop groups such as TWICE, BLACKPINK, EXO, BTS, SEVENTEEN, GFRIEND, Red Velvet, and SNSD. Lately, I've also been into bands like Cup of Joe and Oh! Caraga. In the P-pop scene, I admire BINI, SB19, GAT, P!xie, and Raya, who are making waves with their great music. Don't forget to stream MULTO by Cup of Joe!

CALLIGRAPHY

I first explored calligraphy at 12 with just the basics‚Äîsimple strokes and letters. Back then, it was something I'd do whenever I had a moment, using dual pens or colored pencils. A turning point came when my mom returned from abroad with jars of vibrant color pens, acrylic dual pens, and other supplies, sparking my curiosity even further. With these new tools, I began experimenting and gradually refined my technique. Today, my calligraphy is more polished and expressive, and it remains a joyful escape where I celebrate the art of beautiful writing.

DANCING

Dancing has also been one of my hobbies, but I don't do it as much anymore because I'm busy. I started dancing when I was a kid, taught by my Older cousins and Tita's. ever since I learned to dance, i would perform whenever there were youth activities at our church. In school, I would also occasionally lead dance exercises after the flag ceremony. My cousin and I used to cover K-pop dances for fun, but we never uploaded them because we were too shy. During the pandemic, dancing became one of our ways to stay active and fight boredom, though I wouldn,t say I,m great at it‚ÄîI just enjoy it.
